4-Year Cycle i
Bitcoin follows a roughly 4-year cycle tied to its "halving" events (when new BTC supply gets cut in half). H = Halving, Y1 = Year 1 recovery, MT = Mid-term consolidation, PH = Peak/euphoria phase. Knowing where we are helps set expectations for what typically comes next.

BTC Dominance i
The percentage of total crypto market cap that belongs to Bitcoin. When dominance rises, money is flowing into BTC and away from altcoins. When it drops, altcoins are outperforming ("alt season"). Cowen watches this closely — high dominance often means BTC is the safer bet.

Unemployment & Jobless Claims i
Unemployment rate is the percentage of people actively looking for work but can't find it. Initial claims are how many people filed for unemployment benefits that week — a spike means sudden layoffs. These are the earliest warning signs of economic trouble and directly impact markets.

Housing Market i
Housing is a major economic driver. Mortgage rates control affordability — higher rates mean fewer buyers and slower growth. Housing starts show builder confidence, months of supply indicates buyer vs. seller advantage (6 months = balanced), and the Case-Shiller index tracks actual home price trends nationwide.

Cowen's Macro Rules
DXY: Strong dollar = headwind for BTC. Weak dollar = tailwind.
Fed: Rate cuts = bullish (with lag). Rate hikes = headwinds.
Oil: Oil spikes end business cycles. Sharp rally = bearish signal.
Yield Curve: Inversion = recession warning. Un-inversion = imminent.
